--- notes: | Images are set up with a `max-width` of 100% to help with easy responsiveness. Turn this off with `.img--no-max`. Center images with `.img--centered` and make an *square* image crop to a circle using `.img--circle`. If for some reason you want to protect an image from being downloaded via a right-click, add a `div.image-protector` around the image. While not foolproof for folks who know what they're doing, this prevents the average end user from being able to right-click on the image. ---

--- notes: | Button styles are native on the `button` tag but can also be applied to any element by adding `.btn` to it, particularly for `a` and `input` that need to look like buttons. There are several color variations: * default is a "neautral" grey coloring * `.btn--success`, `.btn--nav`, `.btn--primary`, `.btn--secondary`, `.btn--danger`, `.btn--alert`, `.btn--warning`, and `.btn--info`. --- Submit

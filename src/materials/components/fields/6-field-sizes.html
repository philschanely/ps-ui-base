--- notes: | You can set the size of input fields as follows: * `.input--sm` -- Much smaller than a regular field and good for numbers up to just a few digits * `.input--md` -- A little smaller than a regular field * (normal inputs are this size) * `.input--lg` -- Occupies *most* all of the space left after a label * `.input--xl` -- Occupies *almost all* of the space left after a label * `.input--full` -- Occupies *all* of the space left after a label You also have the option to make a field fill the whole row and omit the label. In such a case add `.field--full` on the `.field` container and the contained controls will fill the row by default. You can still stack on field sizes on the controls as desired. Use this when you don't have a label and want the input to fill the *entire* row. ---

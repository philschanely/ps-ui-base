--- notes: | While a simple form might typically have a single list of fields, if several lists are desired you can group them inside `.form__group` elements. Optionally, add `.form__heading` above each group as well. Related to this, add `.form__description` elements to provide summaries where needed inside a form. Finally, add form-wide feedback by adding `.form__feedback` anywhere desired, but most logically in the `.form__footer`. ---
